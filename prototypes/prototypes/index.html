Available prototypes

Below are draft materials being co-designed and refined through the AATD PR workshops. Files are for research and service-improvement use only.

  • Trait→Plan Interactive Checklist (A4) — clinician form mapping Treatable Traits → assessment → action → follow-up.
    Includes live fields for completion and print/save options.
  • Trait→Plan PDF (A4) — static version for offline or paper use.
  • Exercise Passport (A6) — patient booklet with targets, RPE (0–10), and progression rule (+5–10%/week when within target and no red flags).
  • Stay-Active Kit (cards set) — includes two-week ramp plan, pacing/breathing cues, resistance-band basics, habit tracker, relapse/flare plan, and oxygen setup checklist.
  • Better Living with Exercise booklet — background reading and context for participants (open-access material from the systematic review).

Note: These files may change following feedback from co-design workshops and usability testing.

How these will be tested

  • Complete the Trait→Plan checklist using the “Imran” case; assess clarity and time-to-complete.
  • Review the Exercise Passport for layout, RPE scaling, and progression rule usability.
  • Evaluate which Stay-Active Kit cards are most useful and identify missing elements.
