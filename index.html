Welcome

We’re co-designing practical tools to personalise pulmonary rehabilitation (PR) for people with Alpha-1 Antitrypsin Deficiency (AATD), guided by a Treatable Traits (TT) framework and Experience-Based Co-Design (EBCD).

This site shares prototypes, workshop materials, and outputs from the PR-for-AATD research project at the University of Birmingham.

Key Outputs

  • Stay-Active Kit (cards set)

Project Builds On

  • Systematic Review – Chronic Obstr Pulm Dis, 2024
  • Cohort Analysis – Thorax, 2025
  • Qualitative Interviews – ERS Open Research, 2025
  • Delphi Consensus – Three Rounds (2024–25)
