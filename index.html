Welcome

This project aims to co-design and test practical tools that help personalise pulmonary rehabilitation (PR) for people with Alpha-1 Antitrypsin Deficiency (AATD). It builds on a multi-method programme including a systematic review, cohort analysis, qualitative study, and Delphi survey.

Key Outputs

  • Visual abstract
  • Trait→Plan checklist (A4)
  • Exercise Passport (A6)
  • Stay-Active Kit (cards set)

Next Steps

Upcoming co-design workshops will explore usability and acceptability of prototype tools. Feedback from participants will guide the final design of the AATD-tailored PR toolkit.
