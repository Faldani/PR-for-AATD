  • Trait→Plan checklist (A4) — clinician sheet mapping Treatable Traits → assessment → action → follow-up.
  • Exercise Passport (A6) — patient booklet with targets, RPE (0–10), and progression rule (+5–10%/wk if within range).
  • Stay-Active Kit (cards) — two-week ramp, good/bad day cues, breathing & pacing, resistance-band basics, habit tracker, relapse/flare plan, oxygen setup checklist.
  • Better Living with Exercise booklet — background reading for participants.

Note: Files labelled above may be placeholders during co-design and will be updated after each session.

How these will be tested

  • Complete Trait→Plan using the “Imran” case; time-to-complete and clarity checks.
  • Review Passport wording/layout; confirm RPE targets and progression rule usability.
  • Evaluate which Stay-Active cards are most useful and what’s missing.
